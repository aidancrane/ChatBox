First and Last name

First and last name should be no longer than 30 characters each and should not have any spaces or special text in them

What should we call you

This is your 'nickname' and can be customised at any time, unlike your first and last name. It must be no longer than 16 characters and can contain numbers, letters and spaces. It will appear when you login every time.

Email

Your email address should be no longer than 60 characters and should match the one below it. Make sure it is correct incase we need to contact you, however we do try not to.

Password

Your password should be very hard to guess, we also have blacklisted some of the more easier passwords. all passwords are encrypted with SHA-1 and must be a minimum of 8 characters long, we do not have any special character requirements.
